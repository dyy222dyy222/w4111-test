Platform

This page could help platform implement coupon marketing.

Platform could perform queries on 3 metrics: number of coupons used and shift of consumer behaviors(reservation times and consumption amount) with and without coupon, and number of orders in different cities.

Platform Management

Percentage of Coupon Used

This query would compute the used coupon/coupon issued during the selected period.




Comparison with and without Coupon

This query would compare 2 groups of customers: who had used coupon at least once and who had never used coupon from 2 metrics, average number of reservations and average consumption amount.




Number of Reservations in Each City

This query would return the number of orders of each city in a descending order for the selected period.
